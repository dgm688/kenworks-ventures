What We Do

Insulation, Refractory & Generator Servicing Supplies in Nairobi

From lagging a steam line to lining a furnace to keeping your generator running — Kenworks covers the full thermal, refractory and power stack across Kenya.

Thermal & Acoustic Insulation

Materials that hold heat in, keep heat out, and kill noise — for industrial plant and buildings alike.

  • Rockwool, fiberglass & ceramic fiber — rolls, batts, pipe lagging
  • Armaflex, calcium silicate, vermiculite, styrofoam & PE roof foil
  • Knauf acoustic rolls, foam wedges & panels

Refractory Materials

Fire- and heat-resistant products rated up to 2000°C for the most demanding high-temperature builds.

  • 40%, 70%, zirconia & insulating firebricks, slabs & splits
  • Maxheat A/K castables, Maxlyte 11, Fondu, mortar & acid-resistant brick
  • Ceramic fiber boards, blankets, ropes, cloth & yarn
View all refractory products →

Electrical Safety & Power

Electrical insulation and power-side support to keep your operation safe and running.

  • Duratuf H.V insulating rubber mats — Class 0–4, up to 50kV (ASTM D178)
  • Gland packing, steam gaskets & sealing solutions
  • Generator servicing, repair & spares — 1.5 to 3100 kVA
