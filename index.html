What We Do

Three divisions, one engineering partner.

From lagging a steam line to lining a furnace to keeping your generator running — Kenworks covers the full thermal and power stack.

Thermal & Acoustic Insulation

Materials that hold heat in, keep heat out, and kill noise — for industrial plant and buildings alike.

  • Rockwool & mineral wool — batts, rolls, preformed pipe lagging
  • Saint-Gobain fiberglass / glasswool, Armaflex pipes & sheets
  • Calcium silicate, mill board, aluminium cladding

Refractory Materials

Fire- and heat-resistant products rated up to 1760°C for the most demanding high-temperature builds.

  • 40% & 70% alumina firebricks, insulating bricks, fire slabs
  • Maxheat castable cements, refractory mortar, Fondu cement
  • Ceramic fiber boards, blankets, cloth & modules

Generator Service & Parts

Servicing, repair, overhaul and spares for petrol & diesel generators — any make, any model.

  • Full range from 1.5 kVA up to 3100 kVA
  • Engine overhauls & preventive maintenance
  • Genuine spare parts supply
